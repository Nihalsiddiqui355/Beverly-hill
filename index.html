LOCATION AND CONNECTIVITY
Prime Location & Easy Connectivity

Located just 1.5 hours from South Mumbai and close to the upcoming Navi Mumbai International Airport, Beverly Hills Karjat offers easy access to all major destinations while being a perfect getaway from the city's hustle.

1.5 Hour Drive

Enjoy a scenic 1.5-hour drive from South Mumbai to Karjat via the new Atal Setu. The Panvel-Karjat Corridor will soon cut travel time to just 60-75 minutes.

Air Travel

The new Navi Mumbai Airport will open the "Berkshires of Mumbai" to the world. A quick 1.5-hour drive from the airport brings you to this serene destination.

Train Connectivity

Karjat is seamlessly connected via the Central and Harbour Lines. Soon, air-conditioned trains will make reaching Karjat a hassle-free and comfortable journey.
